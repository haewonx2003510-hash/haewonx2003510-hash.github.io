01 — About Me

Designing at the
edge of culture

I am a fashion design student at Sungkyunkwan University, where I explore the intersection of garment construction, visual identity, and emerging technology. My practice is driven by a curiosity for how clothing communicates ideas before a single word is spoken.

From conceptual runway research to AI-generated visual culture in fashion, I work across physical and digital materials — always asking how aesthetics shape meaning. I believe design is an act of critical thinking made tangible.
